Enrollment Information

Fall 2020 Quarter
Note: Because of the uncertainty surrounding fall enrollment due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the fall Schedule of Classes, which would normally be published in May, will be delayed until the end of the spring quarter. Enrollment appointments for fall have also been pushed back, however, in an effort to give students more time to plan for fall, dates for enrollment appointments for new and continuing students have been set.

Student Responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of your enrollments. Your instructors, academic advisers, and the Office of the Registrar cannot enroll on your behalf. Be sure to verify your classes and grade options prior to the enrollment deadlines listed in the Academic and Administrative Calendar.
